## install

http://people.cas.sc.edu/rorden/mricron/install.html

1. Download latest [.zip file](https://www.nitrc.org/frs/?group_id=152) (lx.zip) on 2016-10-12 06:37
2. Unzip the software, rename the name of folder to 'mricron'.
3. Double-click on the 'YOUR/PATH/mricron/mricron' to launch the program.
